I built Nutrition Rx because the nutrition tools I saw being used weren’t designed for real life chaos... and they weren’t designed for the individual.

As a junior doctor, I’ve seen how hard it is to stay consistent with nutrition when life is busy and unpredictable and how much easier it gets with the right structure.

Nutrition Rx is built around a personalised macro calculator that adapts every recipe to your specific needs, goals, and preferences making nutrition flexible, practical, and actually sustainable.
